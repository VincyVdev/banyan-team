Banyan

Consumer fintech for NRIs and OCIs

Banyan is a consumer fintech platform for NRIs and OCIs, focused on simplifying how people manage money across countries. We’re building a single, digital-first experience that brings together banking, payments, and financial workflows that today feel fragmented and difficult to navigate.
